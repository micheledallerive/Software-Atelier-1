Decade Overview

During the 7th Decade of the 19th century, many great progresses were made in the entire field of computing.

There was an rapid and significant improvement of the hardware, for example with the introduction of the first microprocessors and then later its mass-production or semiconductor memory, witch allowed computers to become much more powerful, affordable to the general public and also allowed to create computers with specific useses such as the super computer cray-1.

A similar significant improvement was found in the software and different programming languages, for example with the creation of C, witch incorporated the Unix, or the creation of the field of Artificial Intelligence.

All this lead to a shift of the use of computers in the eyes of the general public. Computers went from expensive machines that needed to be shared, to machines that could link over networks, to computers that connected with networks of networks (thanks to the invention of the internet), to personal computers for just personal use.
