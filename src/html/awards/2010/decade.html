Decade Overview

During the 2010s decade, computing makes huge progress in machine learning as long as deep neutral networks, both becoming a foundamental component of modern computing. Important is to mention the acknowledgment of the huge improvements in database systems from Stonebraker, which base was then SQL. During this decade we also see Berners-Lee finally get awarded. Honorable mention is Judea Pearl for his contributions to artificial intelligence by developing algorithms for causal reasoning and the incredible growth of 3D computer graphics.
